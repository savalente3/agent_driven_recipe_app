Chickpea Cucumber Feta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 100g feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Handful of fresh parsley
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  • Drain and rinse the chickpeas, pat dry.
  • Dice cucumber and slice red onion thinly.
  • Combine chickpeas, cucumber and onion in a bowl.
  • Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  • Toss with dressing, top with feta and parsley.
